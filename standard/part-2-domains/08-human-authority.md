## 8.1 Purpose

Human authority governs the approval architecture: which action classes require which form of human approval, escalation paths, override mechanics, and accountable acceptance of outcomes. Unengineered, the failure class is authority laundering: consequential actions execute under an approval that was never actually given at the required level, or approval fatigue degrades every gate to a rubber stamp.
