## 5.1 Purpose

Workflow engineering governs multi-step orchestration: topology, concurrency, checkpoints, idempotency, resumption, and compensation for partially completed work. Unengineered, the failure class is unrecoverable partial execution: side effects applied through step N with no record of N and no compensating path.
