## 4.1 Purpose

Agent engineering governs harness-managed execution contexts that pursue goals across multiple model invocations: identity, role, state, goal binding, delegation to sub-agents, termination conditions, and recovery. Unengineered, the failure class is unbounded agency: contexts that outlive their mandate, delegate without attenuation, or cannot be attributed or terminated.
