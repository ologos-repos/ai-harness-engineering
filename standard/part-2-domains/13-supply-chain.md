## 13.1 Purpose

Supply-chain control governs external dependencies: models, prompts, tools, data sources, packages, connectors, and external services — their provenance, integrity, update policy, and blast radius on compromise. Unengineered, the failure class is transitive trust: a dependency change becomes a harness behavior change with no gate in between.
