## 12.1 Purpose

Operational assurance governs the running system: monitoring, drift detection, anomaly detection, rollback, and incident response for harness-specific failure modes (model regression, tool breakage, policy gaps discovered in production). Unengineered, the failure class is silent degradation: behavior shifts and nobody's dashboard says so.
