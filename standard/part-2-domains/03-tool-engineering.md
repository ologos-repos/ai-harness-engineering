## 3.1 Purpose

Tool engineering governs the capability surface exposed to models: schemas, preconditions, postconditions, permission binding, and side-effect classification (read-only vs. mutating vs. externally visible). Unengineered, the failure class is capability ambiguity: the harness cannot state what a tool can affect, so policy enforcement and evidence capture have nothing sound to bind to.
