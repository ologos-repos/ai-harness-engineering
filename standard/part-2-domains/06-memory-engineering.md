## 6.1 Purpose

Memory engineering governs what persists across sessions: scope, schema, provenance, expiration, staleness handling, and cross-session/cross-principal isolation. Unengineered, the failure classes are stale recall treated as current fact, secrets accreting in durable stores, and cross-context leakage.
